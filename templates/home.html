About Us

We are a group of AUB students striving to create a functional database for a gym. Our mission is to provide a platform where gym enthusiasts can easily manage their memberships, track their progress, and access personalized training and nutrition plans.

Services

Our gym offers a wide range of services to cater to our members' needs. From state-of-the-art workout facilities to personalized training sessions with experienced coaches, we ensure that our members have access to everything they need to achieve their fitness goals.

Membership

If you are a member, you can enjoy exclusive benefits such as unlimited access to all our branches, discounts on personal training sessions, and priority booking for group fitness classes.
